# Thien Mac Thong Van Gioi Star Rail Design

## Purpose

Create a new Vietnamese heavenscreen crossover fanfic inspired by the structure and momentum of `崩铁直播通万界，开局吓跑无惨.txt`, without copying its prose, modern-player opening, or exact reaction sequence.

The book should test a broader `livestream across worlds` setup than the current Belobog-only project while preserving the Star Rail storyline as the narrative core.

## Working Identity

- Working title: `Thiên Mạc Thông Vạn Giới, Khởi Đầu Từ Star Rail`
- Working book id: `thien-mac-thong-van-gioi-star-rail`
- Language: Vietnamese
- Genre: heavenscreen / livestream vạn giới / Honkai: Star Rail crossover reaction fanfic
- Primary arc: Herta Space Station opening arc
- Trailblazer: Caelus

## Reference Use

The reference file is used for structural inspiration only:

- A sudden sky screen appears across many worlds.
- Multiple worlds react to a shared Star Rail broadcast.
- The opening creates awe, confusion, threat assessment, and comedic contrast.
- Reaction cuts provide rhythm between cinematic Star Rail scenes.

The new book must not copy:

- The modern player or dorm-room trigger setup.
- Chapter prose or scene wording.
- The exact order and phrasing of world reactions.
- The reference's character jokes as direct equivalents.

## Core Premise

A nameless celestial screen suddenly manifests across countless worlds. It is visible even through walls, eyelids, shelters, and magical barriers, as if projected into perception rather than ordinary sky.

The screen does not announce a modern streamer. It simply opens with the title `Honkai: Star Rail`, then begins showing the Herta Space Station crisis: Kafka and Silver Wolf infiltrating the station, the Stellaron, Caelus's awakening, his first contact with March 7th and Dan Heng, the Antimatter Legion, and the Doomsday Beast.

The worlds initially interpret the phenomenon through their own rules: blood demon arts, ancient magic, cursed techniques, genjutsu, divine intervention, Herrscher-level anomalies, abyssal activity, or unknown imperial weapons.

## Narrative Balance

Use the recommended `HSR core, many-world reaction` structure:

- 60-70% Star Rail broadcast content.
- 30-40% reactions from other worlds.
- Reaction scenes should interrupt at high-signal moments, not after every line.
- Each reaction should reveal the reacting world's worldview or stakes.
- Avoid repeating generic shock lines. Every world should misunderstand or interpret the screen differently.

## Main Reaction Worlds

Use the reference-like world set as the starting pool:

- Demon Slayer
- Frieren
- Akame ga Kill
- Genshin Impact
- Naruto
- Bleach
- Jujutsu Kaisen
- Hunter x Hunter
- Baki
- Honkai Impact 3rd

Not every chapter needs every world. Early chapters can rotate through 5-7 active worlds and leave others as cameo reactions.

## Caelus And March 7th Direction

Increase Caelus x March 7th hints compared to canon, but keep them light and natural.

Allowed:

- March 7th instinctively worrying about Caelus after his awakening.
- Caelus responding awkwardly but sincerely to March's concern or teasing.
- Reaction-world viewers noticing their chemistry and quietly shipping them.
- Small protective beats during combat or crisis.

Avoid:

- Early confession.
- Reframing the whole story as romance.
- Making March 7th lose her canon liveliness or independence.
- Making Caelus overly smooth or possessive.

## Tone

The prose should be Vietnamese, clear, cinematic, and webnovel-readable.

Tone targets:

- Heavenscreen wonder and threat.
- Fast livestream/reaction rhythm.
- Light comedic misunderstanding from cross-world comparisons.
- Respect for HSR canon stakes.
- No excessive meme density.

Proper names should remain in standard canon/Latin spelling where applicable, such as `Caelus`, `March 7th`, `Kafka`, `Silver Wolf`, `Herta`, `Dan Heng`, `Muzan`, `Frieren`, `Mavuika`, and `Mei`.

## Opening Chapter Shape

Chapter 1 should establish the phenomenon before the broadcast fully begins.

Suggested structure:

1. Open in one high-tension world, preferably Demon Slayer with Muzan reacting as if attacked by an unknown power.
2. Cut quickly to several contrasting worlds: Frieren treating it as ancient magic, Genshin suspecting abyssal or divine activity, Jujutsu suspecting a barrier or curse, Honkai Impact 3rd sensing a catastrophic anomaly.
3. Establish that the screen cannot be blocked and can be perceived directly.
4. Display the title `Honkai: Star Rail`.
5. Begin the Herta Space Station broadcast with Kafka/Silver Wolf or the station exterior.
6. End on a hook that the screen is about to reveal the birth of a new Trailblazer.

## Early Arc Direction

The first arc should follow Herta Space Station in order:

- Celestial screen manifests across worlds.
- Herta Space Station appears.
- Kafka and Silver Wolf infiltrate.
- Stellaron is introduced.
- Caelus awakens.
- March 7th and Dan Heng find him.
- Antimatter Legion escalates the crisis.
- Doomsday Beast creates the first major power-scaling shock across worlds.

## Success Criteria

The new book setup is successful if:

- It can be created as a separate Vietnamese book without disturbing the existing Belobog test book.
- The creative brief clearly encodes the no-copy rule and the no-modern-player choice.
- Chapter 1 opens with a distinct voice while preserving the desired reference rhythm.
- Caelus x March 7th hints are present but not dominant.
- Proper names are preserved in standard form.
- The first generated chapter passes basic checks for truncation, language, and forbidden transliteration issues.

## Out Of Scope

- Copying or translating the reference novel chapter-by-chapter.
- Building a new UI or visual workflow.
- Importing the entire reference text into RIS as canon.
- Solving all long-term crossover continuity before chapter 1.
